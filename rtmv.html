Description

Edward Carter Properties are pleased to present this very well maintained corner aspect two bedroom two bathroom apartment located in the heart of Media City, Salford Quays. The apartment is situated on the twelfth floor and briefly comprises of an entrance hallway with two separate storage spaces, a bright open plan living area and kitchen with floor to ceiling windows, which offer stunning views of the Quays and benefits from plenty of natural light. The property has two double bedrooms, one with en-suite, and a seperate main bathroom. The apartment is offered fully furnished.

X1 Media City goes above and beyond with a 24-hour on-site gym, available for tenant use at no extra cost. Secure bike storage and an additional overnight security guard at the reception every evening, 7 days a week, to ensure your convenience and safety. Ideally located in the heart of the Quays, it has great access to local amenities, with a Sainsburys local in the building next door. It also has great transport links with Harbour City Metrolink right on the doorstep. It also has easy access to motorway links with the M602 only five minutes away.

EPC Rating is C. Council Tax Band is C.
